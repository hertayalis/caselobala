Post Malone Shuts Down Rumors that He is Doing Drugs after Fans Show Concern Over His Weight Loss!

Post Malone requests his fans not to be concerned about his weight loss. source: Page Six

  • Post Malone’s fans are showing their concern over the singer’s dramatic weight loss.
  • Many even started saying that Malone is doing drugs which resulted in his weight loss.
  • Malone took to social media to tell fans that he is happier and healthier than ever before.

Post Malone assures fans he is not on drugs

Post Malone is rubbishing rumors that he is on drugs after his fans showed concern over his extreme weight loss.

The rapper whose real name is Austin Richard Post insists he is ‘‘happy and healthy and nothing is amiss.”

Sources close to the Congratulations rapper said that his fans do not need to be scared because “he’s not on drugs or sick.”

The sources further claimed that he had to gain some weight for a movie but has since been following a strict diet and working out.

Moreover, the rapper is also continuously performing a 90-minute set while on tour since September which is also a main factor for his weight loss.

Post’s dad Rich Post also came in defense of his son’s new appearance after drastic weight loss on social media.

He commented on a fan page’s Instagram post, writing,

“Healthiest he’s been in years! Mentally and physically,”

The Sunflower singer shocked his fans when a video of him performing I Fall Apart in Sydney, Australia, looking very slim went viral on social media.

One fan tweeted,

“Just saw a video of Post Malone in a show. He looks bad. Jerking and looks like he lost weight. I hope he’s ok. That’s my dude,”

Another concerned fan added,

“I’m a bit worried that you lost so much weight.. I’m not used to it. Stay healthy please,”

Malone credits his fiancee for saving his life

Some fans came to the rapper’s defense saying that it is his way nature of performing songs with lots of emotion and “isn’t doing drugs anymore.”

One supportive fan tweeted,

“Apparently Post Malone can’t sing his own song with sentiment without people thinking he’s on drugs. Literally this songs [sic] is sooo sentimental I don’t know how anyone could sing this son [sic] with no expression,”

Another Twitter user wrote,

” malone isn’t doing hard core drugs anymore, that’s why his music has changed so drastically and he looks so much healthier. he’s singing with passion now if he was stiff then it’s like ???? but how else is he supposed to sing i fall apart?”

Last year, Post revealed that his unidentified fiancee was always there to support him and help him battle a rough battle with alcoholism.

Talking about his future wife, Malone spoke to Howard Stern Show on June 13,

“I just have super beautiful people around me, and I met a really beautiful person that made me feel like a human being again,”

“It’s the guidance out of the dark into the light … She saved my f–king life — it’s pretty epic.”

Malone welcomed a baby girl with his fiancee last year.
