Kodi Lee (born July 7, 1996) is an American Singer, Musician, Reality Television Personality, Instagram Star, and Musician from South Carolina. He is famous for being the first contestant on the reality show “America’s Got Talent 2019” who received a golden buzzer.

Furthermore, the 22-year-old Kodi was the last singer to perform in the first episode of AGT Season 14. He performed on a legendary song named “A Song For You” by Leon Russell. In fact, Lee also played the piano during his incredible performance.

Blind & Autistic Singer in America’s Got Talent Show

The singer succeeded in getting the golden buzzer from Gabrielle Union (American actress, activist, and author). All four judges Howie Mandel, Julianne Hough, Union, and Simon Cowell gave him a standing ovation after his breathtaking show.

Moreover, Lee is blind and autistic. His parents discovered that he was diagnosed when Kodi was just 4-year-old. However, his mother told that playing and listening to music has saved her son’s life.

Kodi Lee was born with optic nerve hypoplasia. He also sustained a life-saving surgery when he was just 5 days old. But this doesn’t stop him to pursue his passion.
