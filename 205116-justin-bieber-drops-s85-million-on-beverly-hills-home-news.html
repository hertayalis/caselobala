Justin Bieber Drops $8.5 Million On Beverly Hills Home

 Posted on June 30, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  133 words  |  Aldo Pusey

At the top of the month, Justin Bieber and Hailey Baldwin were seen home shopping in the Los Angeles neighborhood of Encino. At the time they were scoping out $13 million homes in the boujee community but now The Blast   reports that the Bieber's have finally settled on a home in Beverly Hills instead.

The publication details how Justin dropped a cool $8.5 million on a 6,100 sq. ft home with five bedroom and seven bathrooms. The luxurious crib was built in 1932 and renovated by Hollywood production designer Charles Infante, “designed for entertaining.” The layout of the home includes a "private courtyard with fire feature, seating area, and olive trees." As well, there's also a "wood-paneled library, sunken family room, and gourmet kitchen, which opens up to the rear grounds and infinity edge pool with cabana."
