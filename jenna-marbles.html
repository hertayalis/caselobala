Jenna Marbles (born September 15, 1986) is an American Youtuber, Actress, Comedian and, Social Media Sensation from Rochester, New York. She is popular for her self-entitled YouTube channel with 19+ million subscribers. Furthermore, the 32-year-old star is the second most subscribed female YouTube personality.

Jenna is the first social media star to have a wax figure in Madame Tussauds Museum in New York City. Moreover, the actress started her career with Barstool Sports. She penned for their female-oriented website named “StoolLaLa“.

Meanwhile, Marbles also started her YouTube channel in April 2006. She uploads videos like Vlogs, reaction videos, lifestyle, viral game challenges, makeup, comedy sketches and much more. Her first viral video was “How To Trick People Into Thinking You’re Good Looking”. This video gained 5+ million views within the first week of upload. As of 2019, the video has 68+ million views.

In addition, Jenna made a debut on the television with the show called “The Moms View” in 2011. She also acted in season 2 of “Epic Rap Battles of History” as Eve. The actress appeared in episode 13, “Adam vs. Eve”. In fact, Jenna made her debut in the films with the movie “Smosh: The Movie” as herself.

Profile, Parents & Early Life

This YouTuber was born on the 15th of September 1986 in Rochester, New York, United States. At present, Jenna Marbles’ age is 32 years old. Her father is a chemist and her mother “Deborah Mourey” helps her to operate her channel properly.

Furthermore, she and her older brother Devon Mourey grew up in in the city on Lake Ontario. The YouTuber completed her school education from Brighton High School in 2004. Later, she graduated from Suffolk University and received a Bachelor of Science degree in Psychology.

Moreover, she then enrolled at Boston University. She post-graduated with a Master of Education degree in Counseling and Sports Psychology. During her college days, the actress did odd jobs like working at a tanning salon, bartending, and go-go dancing at nightclubs.

Dating History, Boyfriend & Spouse

The 32-year-old comedian started her relationship with her ex-partner Willy. They were both in high school together. After dating for almost 4 years, the former couple parted their ways.

Later, in 2008, Jenna started her affair with fellow YouTube star Max Weisz. The love partner used to make appearance their respective social media. In fact, Max also featured on her YouTube channel many times.

Furthermore, Marbles decided to break up from him when she found out that Weisz was cheating on her. She also made it very clear to her fans about their split with her tweet in April 2013.

Currently, Jenna is romantically linked with her boyfriend “Julien Solomita”. She started dating Julien in the early months of 2013. He is also a social media personality and a gamer.

In November 2016, Jenna and her beau Julien were involved in a car accident on a freeway. They were returning from a vacation trip. Fortunately, the couple survived without any serious injuries.
