MUSIC: Sojaboy Zara

 Posted on June 20, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  167 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Talented and versatile Nigerian recording artiste and songwriter, Sojaboy is here with a new melodious single labeled; “Zara”.

The output was taken off his extended play captioned; “King Africa”. The body of works comes in a package of six solid and mind-blowing tracks with no guest artiste.

‘King Africa’ extended play happens to be his first official project of the year (2021) after closing 2020 with a beautifully crafted and still trending song, “Fire”.

Sojaboy is young dynamic Nigerian artiste who has dedicated himself to bring forth his God-given talent by doing music. He has been in the music industry for quite a while.

Sojaboy has been constantly tagged as one of the very talented acts to watch out for in the coming years within the Nigerian music circles.
