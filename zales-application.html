Do you want to work for a company where you will be surrounded by diamonds?

If so, then it may be time to start working on your Zales Application Online: Jobs & Career Info. Zales is the largest diamond retailer in North America and boasts more than 750 different locations.

The company was established in 1924, and the first store was opened in the city of Wichita Falls in Texas. Zales is famous for offering affordable yet high-quality jewelry and excellent customer service.

If you are ready to start working at Zales, the following information will make sure you secure your dream job.

Facts About Working At Zales

You need to be at least eighteen years old to work at Zales. The company’s stores are open from 10:00 am to 9:00 pm from Monday to Saturday, and 12:00 am to 6:00 pm on Sundays. College students who want to gain work experience are encouraged to take on part-time evening and weekend shifts.

Zales Job Opportunities

There are various job opportunities at Zales for applicants who have a love for jewelry and good customer service skills. Entry-level candidates are provided with special training, while there are also different types of jobs for career professionals. Let’s take a look at some of the key job opportunities at Zales and the types of duties that go with them.

Jewelry Consultant

This is a sales role that involves working in one of the company’s stores and assisting customers with purchases. Jewelry consultants need to have excellent customer service skills and the ability to speak knowledgeably about different types of jewelry. Additional duties include cleaning display cases, managing inventory, and handling point-of-sale transactions.

This role is ideal for entry-level candidates and employees who can choose full-time or part-time hours. The average salary for a jewelry consultant at Zales is $11 per hour. However, employees who regularly meet and exceed their sales goals are also provided with a range of performance-based bonuses.

Traveling Jewelry Sales Associate

These sales associates are responsible for attending special jewelry events. They need to set up a Zales stall and arrange the jewelry so that it appeals to potential customers. They need to answer any questions that customers may have and assist them with their purchases.

It is necessary to be very knowledgeable about Zales jewelry and able to work in a noisy environment. Traveling jewelry sales associates also need to protect the jewelry during events and while they are traveling. The average salary for a Zales traveling jewelry sales associate is $26,000 per year.

Assistant Store Manager

This role involves assisting the store manager with a wide range of duties as needed. This includes creating the employee work schedule each week and depositing daily receipts. The assistant store manager makes sure that store associates have the necessary skills to perform their tasks well.

Each week, the assistant store manager prepares a detailed report for the store manager. Therefore, it is necessary to have good attention to detail to do this job. The average salary for an assistant store manager at Zales is $33,000 per year.

Store Manager

The store manager is responsible for overseeing a single Zales store. They need to focus on creating special promotions to attract new customers and maximize profits. Store managers also need to be available to sign off on large customer purchases and order new stock as needed.

Other duties include interviewing, hiring, and training new store employees. The starting salary for a Zales store manager is around $44,000 per year. Store managers who have the necessary skills and experience can expect to earn at least $50,000 per year.

Additional Benefits of Working at Zales

All Zales employees are provided with paid time off and impressive employee discounts on jewelry. Full-time employees are also provided with a range of additional benefits. These include medical insurance, dental and vision plans, paid time off, and access to special retirement plans.

How To Apply?

All of the current job opportunities at Zales can be found in the careers section of the company’s website. It is important to make sure that you have the required skills and background for the role before applying. To do this, simply click on the job title and read the full job description.

It is then possible to click on the apply button and start filling out the Zales application online. Make sure that you complete each section in as much detail and take the time to edit it. It is also important to highlight any previous sales experience and the ability to work a range of shift patterns.

Update your resume…

Before submitting the application form, make sure that your resume is up to date and fits the job. After submitting your application form and resume, it will be reviewed by a hiring manager. A hiring manager will then call or email suitable candidates to arrange an interview in a Zales store.
