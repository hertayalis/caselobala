Will there be a Toilet-Bound Hanako-Kun season 2 or is it finished?

 Posted on July 22, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  505 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Anime fans all over loved it when Toilet-Bound Hanako-Kun hit screens all around the world. Nene Yashiro is like any first-year high school girl. She is nervous, excited, and wants a boyfriend. In order to meet her future boyfriend, Yashiro decides to call on the most famous Wonder, Hanako-san of the Toilet, to try to help her out.

It turns out that the spirit that Yashiro had sought for a boyfriend was plotting to spiritually tie her to him and make her become his assistant. Yashiro and Hanako-san fight evil spirits and have to maintain a balance between both the spirit world and the real world. Yashiro also learns about her past and how she connects to the spirit world, as well as the secrets that Hanako-san carries with him.

Will Toilet-Bound Hanako-Kun get a season two?

Unfortunately for anime fans of the show, there have been no formal announcements by either the studio or writers of the show telling viewers to expect another season. There also has not been any sort of announcement on Twitter or any social media regarding the status of the show by the show pages.

What is making fans speculative is the fact that the show was so popular amongst both fans and critics, thus making it seem inevitable that another season would be coming up.

Although there has been no confirmation about a second season, it has been confirmed that there will be a reboot. It is entitled After-School Hanako-Kun and will be airing on October 12, 2023.

What would take place in the second season?

Season one finished off with a lot of unanswered questions, so the writers were obviously planning for a second season to be able to address them. Although there is still no confirmation on whether or not there will ever be a second season to the show, the new reboot has the opportunity to include what the original show could not.

The episodes are 10 minutes long and are set to continue to uncover the mysteries of Kamone Academy, as well as Nene and Hanako-kun’s quest to keep things going well between the students and the supernatural. The show is said to have the characters playing games with one another, getting sick, changing genders, and uncovering huge secrets.

Much of the cast from the original show will be returning and the assistant director will be stepping up as the official director for the spinoff. This indicates that the short episodes will have a similar feel to the original show.

In addition to what has been confirmed already, there is a fan theory that suggests that Yashiro and Hanako-kun might make an appearance in the real world.

Another theory suggests that the writers will be trying to expand on Yashiro and Hanako-kun and their relationship together, as that is what got Yashiro in this predicament in the first place.

The new reboot can answer the questions that viewers are left with, as well as tell a number of new and exciting stories.
